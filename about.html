About

I’m Savina, a Production Assistant with a background in producing film, television and media projects. My journey into production began through acting and creating my own short films, which naturally led me into producing — developing ideas, organising shoots and keeping projects moving even when resources were limited. Those early experiences taught me how to lead, adapt and problem‑solve under pressure.

Throughout university, I worked predominantly as a Producer, taking on responsibilities that closely mirror the role of a Production Assistant: coordinating people, supporting departments, managing logistics and ensuring the day runs smoothly. I’ve gained hands‑on experience across TV studio environments, film sets and documentary shoots, including projects exploring mindfulness, local heritage and creative audio‑visual storytelling.

Creatively, I have a strong interest in videography, camera operating and editing, especially within short‑form media. I enjoy working on advertising content, short films and documentary pieces, and this creative background helps me understand the needs of each department on set.

I work best in structured, fast‑moving production environments where clear communication and teamwork matter. I bring a steady, detail‑focused approach to every set, staying aware of what’s needed and supporting departments efficiently.
