Desc

This documentary explores the rich history and heritage of Delapré Abbey, one of Northampton’s most significant landmarks. Through interviews, visuals, and research, the film traces the Abbey’s transformation from a medieval nunnery to a modern cultural venue. It highlights the people who preserve its legacy and the importance of heritage in connecting past and present.

The Game We Play is a noir‑inspired, poetic short film exploring obsession, morality, and the emotional tension between hunter and hunted. When a detective begins to protect and stop chasing, she gets stuck in the web of indiscretion. The hunt game between criminal and detective turns into a dance of love. Witness the unexpected bond which forms when law and obsession collide.
